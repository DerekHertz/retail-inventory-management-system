Welcome to Derek's Garden Kits & Planters - your one-stop destination for complete gardening solutions and custom planter designs!

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Derek's Garden Kits & Planters began as a passion project to make gardening accessible to everyone, regardless of space or experience level. Derek started crafting custom planter boxes in his garage, combining quality materials with everything needed to create thriving gardens. Today, we've grown into Southern California's premier provider of complete garden kits and handcrafted planters.

What We Offer

  • Complete Garden Kits: Everything you need in one package - planter, soil, drainage materials, plants/seeds, and instructions
  • Custom Planter Boxes: Handcrafted cedar and redwood planters built to your specifications
  • Indoor Garden Systems: Herb gardens, succulent terrariums, and windowsill kits
  • Outdoor Garden Solutions: Raised bed kits, vertical gardens, and container garden setups
  • Garden Components: Premium soils, drainage materials, containers, and hardware
  • Seasonal Kits: Spring vegetable gardens, summer herb collections, and winter indoor growing systems

Why Choose Derek's Kits?

Every kit includes precisely measured components designed to work together perfectly. Our handcrafted planters use sustainable, locally-sourced cedar with weather-resistant finishes. We believe gardening should be enjoyable, not overwhelming - that's why our kits come with step-by-step guides and ongoing support.

Our Commitment

We're dedicated to sustainable gardening practices, using eco-friendly materials and supporting local suppliers. Whether you're a beginner wanting to start your first herb garden or an experienced gardener looking for quality components, we have the perfect solution for your space and skill level.
