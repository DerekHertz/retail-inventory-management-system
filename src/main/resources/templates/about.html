About Derek's Greenhouse

Welcome to Derek's Greenhouse, your premier destination for quality bicycles and expert service!

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Derek's Bike Depot has been serving the Los Angeles cycling community with quality two-wheelers! What started as a small repair shop has grown into a full-service bicycle store.

What We Offer

  • Wide selection of bicycles for all ages and skill levels
  • Professional bike repairs and maintenance
  • Custom bike builds and modifications
  • Cycling accessories and gear
  • Expert advice from certified mechanics

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the best cycling experience possible through quality products, expert service, and genuine care for the cycling community.
